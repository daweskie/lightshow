#define BELA_ADC_RESOLUTION   (1<12)

AD conversion for temperature controlling.

Idea: There are 8 multiplexed channel outside of controller. The analog input is in BELA_ADC_PORT_BIT of BELA_ADC_PORT There 3 bit of channel selection in BELA_ADC_CHANNEL_MASK bits of BELA_ADC_CHANNEL_PORT

The software have to select each channel and measure it.
